PL Connors

A science fiction thriller set against the back drop of life on a space colony, follow Private Lawman Lawrence Connors and investigative journalist Nicole Morris as they solve the strange murder of Martin Morris, a case that gets them entangled into the seedy underworld of the space colony Trine. Follow these intrepid heroes on their journey for truth in a harsh world, in this first novella written by new author Lewis Fields.
